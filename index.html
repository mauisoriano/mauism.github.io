My Travels

Exploring the Philippines

Camiguin Island

+

Together with my family, we explore the "Island Born of Fire", Camiguin.
Camiguin is a small yet adventure-packed destination in Northern Mindanao. Known for its volcanic landscapes, white-sand islands, waterfalls, and hot springs, Camiguin is a paradise for nature lovers and history enthusiasts alike.

Bukidnon

+

With a long-weekend during the first week of November, I travelled to Bukidnon with my family.
Traveling through Bukidnon offers a mix of adventure, cool mountain escapes, and breathtaking landscapes. Each destination has its own unique charm, making this route perfect for those who love nature and thrill-seeking activities.

Iloilo City

+

As we celebrated my Mom's birthday, we decided to visit the beautiful city of Iloilo.
Iloilo City is a beautiful blend of history and modernity, where the past and present coexist harmoniously. Known as the "Heart of the Philippines", it boasts a rich heritage seen in its well-preserved centuries-old churches, ancestral houses, and historical landmarks.
